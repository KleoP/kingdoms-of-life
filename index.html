7 Kingdoms of Life

This website talks about the characteristics and some representative phyla of each of the 7 kingdoms of life according to the PLoS One Article by Ruggier et al. This classification does not represent evolutionary relationships or a phylogeny, but rather a hierarchial classification that might have practical applications. Life is far more complex and untidy in its evolution than Linnaeus initially described.
